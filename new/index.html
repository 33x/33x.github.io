About

33X, LLC focuses on machine learning and robotics consulting. 33X is currently working on projects such as performing technical due diligence for a large asset manager prior to investment, building an automated solution to cell identification for a biotech company, creating simulations and controllers for consumer drone platforms, building a data distribution network for unstructured finance-related data, advising an agricultural startup on their image recognition product, and developing an office pricing model for a co-working space.

We are always on the lookout for new challenging problems!


Team

Principal
Joshua Joseph has a decade of experience solving real-world problems using the tools of machine learning. He received a B.S. in Applied Mathematics and a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Rochester Institute of Technology and a S.M and Ph.D. from Massachusetts Institute of Technology. At MIT, his Ph.D. work focused on methods for learning models of complex systems from limited data for decision making. He worked on a variety of real-world projects, such as modeling battery health degradation, predicting taxi routes, and autonomous robot interaction with turbulent water flow. Following his Ph.D., Josh co-founded Causality Technologies, a proprietary trading company, focusing on fully automated trading and ML-based strategy discovery.

Principal
Marek Doniec has 10+ years of experience developing autonomous robotic systems for ground-, air-, and underwater applications and has lead multiple efforts in this space. Marek holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering from MIT, a M.S. in Computer Science from Yale University and a Vordiplom (B.S.) in Computer Science from the University of Karlsruhe. He has been an active member of research labs in the United States, Germany, France, and Singapore. Additionally, he has experience in industry, having worked at Rethink Robotics and Google [x], as well as a stint as a quantitative researcher on Wall St.
